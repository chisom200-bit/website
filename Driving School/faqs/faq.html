Frequently Asked Questions

Our FAQs section provides answers to common questions about our driving programs, lessons, schedules, pricing, and licensing process. Find quick, clear information to help you get started on your driving journey with confidence

Will you arrange for a driver's license for me?

Yes, we start processing your license right from the day we present you to DVLA for your learner's license.

Is it compulsory that i go to DVLA for the test ?

Yes, It is only DVLA that can issue a valid driving licemse for student drivers. Therefore the theory and practical test are compulsory

What is the duration of the program?

The regular package last 6 weeks, the express package last 3 weeks and polishing last 4 weeks

Do I need to go through the theory lessons

Yes, it is mandattory by DVLA that student driver should go through 12 topics on defensive driving before obtaining a leaners license.

Do You have a shorter period of completion

The Shorter completion period is 3 weeks which is the express course

I can Drive. Can i get a license Only?

Yes, You can.

I know how to drive but i'm not very good, Can you help me?

You would have to opt for the polishing program

Can I choose a preferred Instructor

Unfortunately No, Instructors are assigned to practical session based on their avaliability
