About Dr. Michael Jones

Dr. Michael Jones is a board-certified radiologist with over 18+ years of experience in diagnostic imaging. He earned his medical degree from Thomas Jefferson University and currently practices in Savannah, Georgia. Dr. Jones is affiliated with several hospitals, including Memorial Health University Medical Center, Coastal Carolina Hospital, and Hilton Head Regional Medical Center. His expertise encompasses a wide range of imaging modalities, with a particular focus on breast imaging and biopsies. Dr. Jones is committed to delivering accurate and timely diagnoses to support optimal patient care.
