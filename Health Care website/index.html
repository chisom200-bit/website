Our Services

General Consultation

General Consultation is often the first step in your healthcare journey. It involves a one-on-one meeting with our medical Practitionals to discuss any health concerns, symptoms, or questions you might have. Whether it’s a mild headache, a persistent cough, or just a general check-up, Our goal is to listen, assess, and guide you toward the right care. We take time to understand your health history and current condition, then provide the best advice, treatment, or referrals tailored to your needs. Your wellness starts with a conversation and we’re here for it.

Peditric Care

Pediatric Care is all about keeping your little ones healthy and happy. From newborns to teenagers, our pediatric services focus on the unique medical needs of children at every stage of growth. Whether it’s routine check-ups, immunizations, treating common illnesses, or tracking development milestones, we provide compassionate and expert care in a friendly environment. We know kids aren’t just small adults, they need special attention, patience, and a gentle touch. Our goal is to support both children and parents with the guidance and care they need to thrive, every step of the way.

Family Planning

Family Planning gives you the power to make informed choices about if and when to grow your family. We offer guidance, support, and a range of safe, confidential services from contraceptives to fertility advice, all tailored to your personal goals and health needs. Whether you're planning for your first child or spacing between children, our caring team is here to help you take control of your reproductive health. It’s not just about prevention, it’s about planning your future with confidence, clarity, and care.

Pharmacy

Pharmacy services are here to make getting your medications simple, safe, and stress-free. Whether you’ve just been prescribed something or need a refill, our team ensures you get the right medication, the right dose, and the right advice. We’re not just about handing out pills, we’re also here to answer your questions, explain how your medication works, and guide you on how to take it properly. From everyday prescriptions to over-the-counter support, we’ve got your back when it comes to your health and wellness. Your peace of mind is part of the care we provide

Immunization

Immunization is one of the safest and most effective ways to protect yourself and your loved ones from serious diseases. From childhood vaccines to adult boosters, our immunization services are designed to keep your immune system strong and ready. We offer a full range of recommended vaccines in a clean, comfortable, and caring environment. Our team is here to answer any questions, ease any worries, and make the process quick and stress-free. By staying up to date with your vaccines, you're not just protecting yourself but you're also helping to protect your whole community too.

Diagnostic Services

Diagnostic Services help us understand what’s really going on with your health. Using modern tools and tests like blood work, X-rays, scans, and lab analysis. We can detect, monitor, or rule out medical conditions quickly and accurately. Whether you're feeling unwell or just doing a routine check-up, these tests provide the answers needed to guide your care. Our skilled team ensures the process is smooth, safe, and explained every step of the way. With the right diagnosis, you can take the right steps and we're here to help you do just that.

Raphael's Health Clinic

At Raphael's Health Clinic, we are committed to providing high-quality, affordable, and compassionate healthcare to individuals and families in our surroundings and all over the world. Our team consists of skilled and dedicated medical professionals who prioritize patient well-being, comfort, and satisfaction. We offer a wide range of services—from routine checkups and preventive care to treatment of illnesses and health education—all tailored to meet your unique health needs. We understand that visiting a clinic can sometimes be stressful, so we aim to create a welcoming and supportive environment where every patient feels heard, respected, and cared for. We believe that good health starts with trust and communication, and we work closely with our patients to ensure they receive the best possible care. Your health is our top priority, and we are honored to be part of your journey toward a healthier, happier life. Thank you for choosing Raphael's Health Clinic.

Location

Raphael's Health Clinic is conveniently located in the heart of Ikeja, at Number 10 Allen avenue, Lagos. making it easy for patients across the area to access quality healthcare. Our facility is easily reachable by public transport and has ample parking space for those arriving by car. We are situated in a safe, welcoming neighborhood, surrounded by essential amenities and services. Whether you're coming in for a routine visit or urgent care, our location is designed for your convenience. Visit us today and experience healthcare that’s close, caring, and committed to your well-being.

Checkups by Our Trained Practitionals

Our checkups are conducted by highly trained and experienced medical professionals who are dedicated to your overall health and well-being. During each visit, our practitioners take the time to carefully assess your condition, listen to your concerns, and provide personalized recommendations tailored to your unique needs. From vital signs and physical examinations to preventive screenings and health advice, we ensure every checkup is thorough, accurate, and patient-centered. With a focus on early detection and wellness, we’re here to help you stay healthy, informed, and in control of your health journey.

Hospitality

We believe great healthcare starts with genuine hospitality. From the moment you walk through our doors, you’ll be greeted with warmth, respect, and care. Our friendly staff is always ready to assist, making sure you feel comfortable and supported throughout your visit. We’ve created a clean, welcoming environment where patients of all ages feel at ease. Your comfort and satisfaction matter to us, and we’re committed to providing a positive, stress-free experience every time you visit.
