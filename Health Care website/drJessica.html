About Dr. Jessica Syemdy

Dr. Jessica is a board-certified pediatrician with over 12 years of experience in providing comprehensive care to children. She holds a Master of Public Health (MPH) and is an International Board-Certified Lactation Consultant (IBCLC), demonstrating her commitment to holistic child and family health. Dr. Jessica is affiliated with Summit Health and practices in New Rochelle, NY. Her clinical interests include pediatric primary care, breastfeeding support, and community health initiatives.
