Our Medical Services

Committed to delivering quality healthcare with compassion and care.

General Consultation

Our General Consultation service provides patients with expert medical advice, routine check-ups, and early diagnosis of health issues. Our experienced doctors take the time to understand your concerns, review your medical history, and offer personalized care. Whether you're feeling unwell or just need a health check, we’re here to help. Schedule an appointment or walk in today—your health is our priority at Raphael's Health Care.

Prediatric Care

Our Pediatric Care service is dedicated to the health and well-being of infants, children, and teenagers. With a team of friendly and skilled pediatricians, we offer routine check-ups, immunizations, growth monitoring, and treatment for childhood illnesses. We create a safe and welcoming environment for every child. At Raphael's Health Care, we care for your little ones like family—because their health and happiness matter most.

Family Planning

Our Family Planning service empowers individuals and couples to make informed decisions about reproductive health. We offer confidential counseling, contraceptive options, fertility advice, and support tailored to your needs. Whether you're planning for a child or preventing pregnancy, our compassionate healthcare providers are here to guide you every step of the way. At Raphael's Health care, we prioritize your choices, privacy, and overall well-being.

Pharmacy

Our in-house Pharmacy offers fast, reliable access to quality medications prescribed by our healthcare providers. Staffed by licensed pharmacists, we ensure accurate dispensing, dosage guidance, and drug interaction checks. We stock a wide range of prescription and over-the-counter medicines at affordable prices. At Raphael's Health Care, your safety and convenience come first—trust us for all your pharmaceutical needs under one roof.

Immunization

Our Immunization service helps protect children and adults from preventable diseases through safe, up-to-date vaccines. We follow national and international vaccination schedules to ensure full coverage, including childhood vaccines, travel vaccines, and booster shots. Our trained nurses provide care in a calm, supportive environment. At Raphael's Health Care, we make immunization easy, safe, and effective—because prevention is the best protection.

Diagnostic Services

Our Diagnostic Services offer accurate and timely testing to support proper diagnosis and treatment. From blood tests and urine analysis to X-rays and ultrasounds, we use modern equipment to deliver reliable results. Our skilled technicians work closely with doctors to ensure each test is conducted with precision and care. At Raphael's Health Care, we provide trusted diagnostics to help you understand your health and make informed decisions.
